I am an Associate Professor at HEC Montréal. Before that, I was an Assistant Professor at Columbia University (2019–2026) and a graduate student at UCSD (2014–2019).

My research interests lie at the intersection of macro, labor, and finance. In ongoing work, I quantify the welfare effects of macroeconomic shocks (saving gluts, tariffs, AI), with an emphasis on redistribution between workers and capitalists.
